Omega Loop Progression

Omega Loop Resets

  • Super Ultra Mega Loop
  • All Previous Looplet Upgrades (except Arrow Upgrades)
  • Lime Charge and Lime Charge EL Upgrades
  • Blue Realm (except Grass-Touch and Blautomation Upgrades)

Omega Loop Unlocks

  • True End - Unlocked at Omega Loop 10
  • Nothing else, as of now

Recommended Looplet Upgrades

Note: There is no Omega Loops per Omega Loop upgrade.

  • Omega Loop 1: Automated
  • Ultra Loop 2: Super Ultra Mega Solarians
  • Ultra Loop 4: Mega Loops per Mega Loops II
  • Ultra Loop 6: Super Ultra Mega Loopier Loops + Iridium
  • Ultra Loop 8: Keep Tier 9 and Restoration + Loops per Loop II

From now on you repeat the Ultra Looplet upgrades on Omega Loops until the end.

  • Omega Loop 2: Omega Solarians
  • Omega Loop 5: Super Mega Loops per Super Mega Loops II (Save for this upgrade as it makes you do a SUML in 20 loops, or 16 with MLpML II upgrade)
  • Omega Loop 6: Omega Loopier Loops
  • Omega Loop 7: Loops per Loop III
  • Omega Loop 8: Omega Keep Tier 9 and Restoration

Tips for Faster Runs

Requires around 100B/1e11 loop multi.

Early Game

Cut grass and then Crystallize, press the Multi Grasshop button, Steelie twice and Galactic. Go to anti realm and Liquefy, then Galactic for GS 18+. Sacrifice to update AGH milestones to AGHGS 30+. Go to Planetoid and Form Ring, then do a Quadrant and Form Ring with Reservatorium and Sacrifice to reach AGHGS 45. Walk up to Ring Break and allocate Dark Fruits into XP and Cosmic.

Planetoid

Get enough Reservatorium for Unnatural Realm autocut and Measure gen. Go for Planetary Tier 20+. Teleport to Unnatural Realm, cut some grass and GJ at 35+ for Supernova (If not enough multi, GJ then allocate lunar power and Dark Fruits into xp then GJ35+). Quickly do a Prestige, and Supernova again for SNT5.

Solarians

Requires at least Super Ultra Mega Solarians.

  1. Spam Allocate One to All, while you gain stages, form a constellation and place a line generator.
  2. Get SNT7 then load your constellation setup and spam Allocate one to all to form the Grasslands portal.
  3. Sunrise once, allocate DSouls and teleport to Grassland.
  4. Go through the Synthesis portal. Start synthesis, upgrade shards and speed, repeat twice then right click all upgrades.
  5. Reach SNT11, allocate to all, allocate DSouls and then sunrise.
  6. Now keep allocating DSouls until you get SNT13. Centralize everything, twilight at stage 480+ and spend all singularity points.
  7. Allocate USouls, check Synthesis until you get SNT14 at stage 700+ then stay on Synthesis until you reach stage ~980.
  8. Immediately go to Floor 1 and loop. Buy the +1 ML upgrade and do a Mega Loop.

Upgrade Details

  • Loops per Loop III: Increases loops normally gotten by +2. Will not keep Loops per Loop II. Useful for SUML recovery.
  • Automated: Keeps All The Realms and Super Ultra Mega Supernova on Omega Loop. Gives Normal, Anti, Space, Unnatural, Planetoid, Supernova autobuyers and EL to supernova/multi-GH/GS/GJ/Planetary.
  • Omega Solarians: Keeps 'Auto Soul I' and SUM Solarians. Auto Soul I is weak, but keeping SUM Solarians gives Divine Absorber, Darkness Arrives, Soul Absorber, Free Compression.
  • Keep Galaxy: Keeps Galaxy Upgrade on Omega Loop. Don't buy this. It's not worth it.
  • Omega Loopier Loops: Multiplies Global Multiplier by 2x while keeping SUM Loopier Loops. Total 6.552x Loop Multiplier.
  • Omega Keep Tier 9 and Restoration: Keeps Keep Tier 9 and Restoration. It is just Keep Restoration for the price of an Omega Looplet.
  • Super Mega Loops per Super Mega Loop II: (3 Omega Looplets). Increases SM Loops on SM Loop by +1. Lets you save 1 SML per SUML.

True End (6.0)

True End is the final reset layer of World 1.

  • Doing the True End will fully reset your Loops, Loop Shop, Blue Realm, and Grassland Progress.
  • Exceptions (Not Reset): Combat Ranks, Gold and Shards (until disconnecting), Special and Rare Mastery tab.
  • Flowers, Rare Flowers, Planet Run, and Time Warps are not reset.
  • It does not reset grassland progress (for some reason).
  • Gives a global loop multiplier of 0.7x per true end (Note: text might be outdated in game).
  • Because every currency is floored (rounded down), you may get 0 of a currency until you get at least a 10/7x multiplier for them.
Note: Reward: 5,000 Flowers (Achievement) and 'Certified Grass Avoider' title.
