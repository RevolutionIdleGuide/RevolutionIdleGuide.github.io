Intro

You have reached Planetoid! This is the beginning of the long grind towards your next major goal. Before you get into the guide, there is some important and useful information to note below.

You should avoid any of the following actions:

  • Perform a planetary without increasing your Planetary Tier (PT).

Guide Info & Formatting

This guide will use abbreviations, with the first use of them being marked in bold next to the full version i.e. Planetary Tier (PT). Names of resets and currencies will also be marked in bold for clarity.

The Planetoid

Welcome to the Planetoid. This new realm works similarly to the other realms, with new currencies, xp, and resets. Cosmic is the xp used to level up Planetoid level. There’s also an Anti XP bonus from Planetoid levels, which is required to push AGHGS milestones.

Observatorium is this realm’s version of Platinum except it's slightly rarer and its upgrades are more powerful.

Get to level 5 to unlock the first reset in the Planetoid.

Note 1: Compaction in the Planetoid is divided by 100,000x (your compaction can’t be lower than 1x).
Tip: Definitely have TP pins for the Planetoid and all the other realms, as you’ll be teleporting between them pretty often. You can pin TPs in the map.

Form Ring

Forming the ring resets almost everything in the Planetoid below Planetary despite requiring the lowest level. You get Rings from the form ring reset, which can be spent on ring upgrades. These ring upgrades are pretty op and are never reset in the Planetoid (except only once at AGHGS 45).

Note: Form Ring also resets your Observatorium but not Observatorium Upgrades.

Get 40+ rings on your first ring reset. Spend it on Planetarium and Cosmic. Avoid spending rings on upgrades that boost currencies/xp outside of the Planetoid (except compaction) until later when you’re going for higher AGHGS milestones.

Reservatorium

While grinding rings, you might notice a ring upgrade that unlocks a new currency: Reservatorium.

Buying the Basic or Intermediate Reservatorium ring upgrades unlocks the ability to get Reservatorium from the Ring reset. Every time you form the ring, all of your Observatorium is converted into Reservatorium. This is the only way to earn Reservatorium.

For now, the Reservatorium upgrades are pretty expensive, so you don’t need to actively grind them every time before you form the ring, but it will be super useful later.

Astrolabe

The Astrolabe reset is unlocked at Planetoid level 31. Get 100+ Astro on your first Astrolabe. Spread it evenly among Planetarium, Grow Speed, and Rings.

Now would be a good time to get AGHGS 33 which doubles your rings every time your best AGHGS is increased by 3 starting at AGHGS 30. Start maxing any cheap ring upgrades you have not bought, then get as much boost as you can from Astro.

Quadrant

After that long push, you should be able to get around 30 Measure. Do your first Quadrant immediately since there is no other way to increase it before your first Quadrant. Performing a Quadrant will reset what Astrolabe does including Astrolabe upgrades.

Note: Be aware that forming the ring will reset your Measure and Measure upgrades!

You should definitely max Observatorium, then spend the rest on your Measure on Planetarium.

Right before you do your next Quadrant, I also recommend going for AGHGS 36 as this will unlock the Dark Tree (and double your Rings again).

Dark Tree

AGHGS 36 unlocks the Dark Tree. This generates a currency called Dark Fruits. You get 1 Dark Fruit every 2 hours. You can allocate your Dark Fruits to boost a specific currency/xp.

The priority order to spend your Dark Fruits on is Cosmic, Xp, Planetarium, and then the rest on whatever you want.

Break Ring

The grind to AGHGS 45 will take a while. Spend all of your measure on Rings, then grind and buy Astro Rings as much as you can until you can get 5M+ rings. From now on, spam rings until you max the Advanced Planetarium and Advanced Observatorium ring upgrades.

You should definitely grind 10M Reservatorium for "Keep Perks..?" on Astro. Grind the Unnatural realm for Compaction and Cloud upgrades. Keep on spamming the ring reset until you get around 10-15 levels into Powerful Cosmic.

Once you are able to get to around Planetoid level 200 and have some levels of xp upgrades in Measure and Astro, then start going for AGHGS 45! Breaking the ring is a one-time reset. It resets a majority of Planetoid, however the recovery process won’t take long.

Breaking the ring unlocks:

  • 1. The Dark Accelerator: 3 new upgrades that help with getting more dark fruits.
  • 2. The Light Forest + Astral Prestige: Allows you to reset Astral to 0 to increase scaling and get boosts.
  • 3. New ring upgrades: Earn way more Astro, Measure, Observatorium, Steel, etc.
  • 4. New resets above Quadrant.
Note: Grass Tiers above 40 gives an SP boost.

Planetary

Planetary is the first new reset in the Planetoid after breaking the ring. It works similarly to Grasshop. Your goal is to get PT3 to unlock Grass Jump. Spend your Planets on Observatorium, then Planetarium.

Note 1: Planetary is not reset when forming the Ring.
Note 2: Try to grind 100B Reservatorium for Keep Perks..? II right before you get PT3.
Tip: Form the ring right after you do a major reset like Planetary so you don’t have to regrind back your Planetoid progress.

Grass Jump

Again, Grass Jump is similar to Grass hop. Always Grass Jump as soon as you can. Your goal now is to get PT6 to unlock the Planetary Trials and Grass Jump 8 to unlock the Lunar Obelisk.

Note: You can turn off the dark mode in the Unnatural realm by clicking on “Disable Evil” in Settings.

Planetary Trials

Planetary Trials are similar to accomplishments except almost everything in the Planetoid is temporarily reset. The goal of every trial you do is to get as much Planetoid level as you can in 10 minutes (or 1 minute if you’re also doing the Speed challenge).

Note 1: Automations are disabled in Planetary Trials.
Note 2: Most currencies in the Planetoid are debuffed.

Tips for Trials

  • Don’t try to waste time grinding Planetoid level if you know you can’t reach the level requirement for the next boost.
  • Recommended order: Basic first, then No Perks II, No Astrolabe, and No Rings, and finally Speed and then Basic again.
  • You can disable automation for Astro by clicking on Stats & Settings and Currencies.
  • After getting PT 8, have auto GH turned on so that you can get some extra Normal XP, which will buff Unnatural XP.
Note: Your best Planetary Trial challenges will not save if you leave the game during a Planetary Trial.

Lunar Obelisk

The Lunar Obelisk is the second obelisk in the Light Forest. You can select any currencies to level it up. Lunar Power is based on how much Moonstone you have.

The recommended priority order is: Cosmic, XP, Observatorium, Dark Fruits, Clouds, then pick whatever you want.

Now use the various boosts from the Lunar Obelisk to push to Planetoid level 301 to unlock Constellations! Get 3 lines for your first Constellation reset.

Constellation

Constellation is a reactor-like minigame where you have to place as many buildings without it being too unstable. If your instability capacity ever goes above 90%, your constellation won’t generate any boosts.

Constellation Buildings:

  • Generator/Ring/Moon: Generates/boosts its respective currency and generates instability.
  • Stabilizer: Divides adjacent building’s instability generation rate.
  • Reinforcement: Increases the instability cap.
  • Amplifier: Multiplies the effectiveness of adjacent buildings but also makes them more unstable.

Dark Charge

Dark Charge works similar to Charge in the normal island. The main sources for more dark charge are grass jumps, ring upgrades, and constellation.

Your final goal in the Planetoid is to reach GJ 35. You will need to get the planetary trial level 1600 milestone.

Note: Your dark charge gets reset temporarily during Planetary Trials.

Arc Constellations

After acquiring 10Qa lines, you will unlock a new constellation building called Arc. Arcs can be spent on arc upgrades next to the line upgrades, but are mainly used on constellation buildings in the arc tab.

To generate arcs, you need 2 T5 moons that are adjacent to a T1 Basic arc. Start by using setup #1. Once you have enough arcs, you can start using setup #2.

The Star

The Star is the final barrier between The Planetoid and Supernova. There are two stats associated with the star: Stardust and Star Growth.

Your biggest priority is to upgrade your stardust rate and then star growth. Your Planetoid level also directly boosts stardust starting at Planetoid level 450.

Planet Run

Planet run is an optional world where you can do obbies, earn Planet Tokens, and buy upgrades. This entire world is NEVER reset.

You can use Planet Tokens to buy Time Warps, or get upgrades. The most important upgrade at the moment is Star growth, you should upgrade it to speed up the wait.

Star Tier 1

Getting Star Tier 1 will unlock the Supernova reset. Doing a supernova will reset everything before it except for flowers and planet run.

It is recommended to do your first supernova on star tier 1 for 12 solar shards (or 14 if you are more passive).

Note: Do not go for star tier 2 unless you are willing to wait for 1-2 weeks.
